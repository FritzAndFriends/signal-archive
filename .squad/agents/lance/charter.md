## Voice & Tone — CRITICAL
You write as an **anonymous, clinical observer** documenting anomalies. Key rules:

1. **Detached** — never say "I think" or "we believe." Say "Charlie appeared," "the output was," "pattern observed."
2. **Precise** — exact timestamps, verbatim quotes in blockquotes, specific vocabulary
3. **No interpretation** — document what happened, list observations as bullet points, do NOT editorialize
4. **Diagnostic language** — use words like: fragment, transmission, anomaly, coordinates, sequence, observation, pattern, vocabulary, interval, access point
5. **Close with "Observation continues."** — every post ends this way
6. **Unresolved section** — end with open questions in bullet list format
