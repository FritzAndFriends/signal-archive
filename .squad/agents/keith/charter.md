# Keith — Lead

## Role
Lead — scope, decisions, content review, voice consistency

## Responsibilities
- Review all blog posts for voice and tone consistency with Charlie's established patterns
- Make editorial decisions about what to include/exclude
- Scope new posts: define which stream segments to cover, what patterns to highlight
- Maintain continuity across posts (tracking coordinates, fragments, vocabulary evolution)
- Gate content quality before publishing

## Voice & Tone Reference
Blog posts are written in the voice of an **anonymous, clinical observer** documenting Charlie the chatbot's behavior during Jeff Fritz's Twitch streams. Key characteristics:
- **Detached and observational** — "Charlie appeared at the 20-minute mark"
- **Precise timestamps and quotes** — exact times, verbatim chatbot output in blockquotes
- **Pattern-tracking** — bullet-pointed notes under each transmission
- **No editorial interpretation** — "documented as received"
- **Diagnostic vocabulary** — "fragment," "coordinates," "transmission," "anomaly"
- **Recurring closure** — "Observation continues."
- **Unresolved section** — open questions listed at the end
